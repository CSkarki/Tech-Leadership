Case Study: ISO 27001 Certification Initiative

Context:
CIBT aimed to become ISO27001 certified to improve data governance and meet international compliance standards.

Role & Leadership:
As Director, I led the enterprise audit readiness and policy alignment effort across business units.

Approach:
	•	Established a cross-functional Risk Control Board
	•	Integrated risk logs into project reporting and Agile ceremonies
	•	Partnered with external auditors and implemented InfoSec controls

Outcome:
	•	Achieved ISO 27001 certification within 9 months
	•	Reduced audit findings by 75% YoY
	•	Created reusable security governance playbooks
